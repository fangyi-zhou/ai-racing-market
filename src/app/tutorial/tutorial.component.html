Introduction

  1. Warning: This is an advanced tutorial for when you're comfortable with car control
  2. It is just one way you could make your AI for the races

Objectives

  1. Make a population of cars, each with a neural network
  2. Make the neural networks have the number of inputs equal to the number of car sensors (10) and the number of outputs equal to the number of car actions (2)
  3. Each epoch, race all the cars. Rank them by their performance on the track, and use a Genetic Algorithm to produce the next generation.
  4. When the AI is trained, save the weights!
